How to Play

  1. The cost to play is $5 for each submission.
  2. On the Picks page: select a winner for each game by clicking that team's logo.
  3. For the last game each week; select a winner and enter the total points that you think will be scored in that game (this is the tie-breaker).
  4. Enter your contact information.
  5. Submit.
  6. You'll receive an email copy of your picks from Formspree. You can see all the picks here at any time. NOTE: this sheet updates every 5 minutes - be patient.
  7. The RESET link at the bottom of the form will reset the entire form (don't click it unless you really mean it...DON'T CLICK IT!).

Prizes

  • 90% of the pot will be paid out weekly. 10% will go into an end-of-season pot.
  • Prize will be awarded for 1st place each week.
  • In the event of a picks tie during the regular season, the total points from the Monday night game will be used to determine the winner. If a tie still exists after total points, that weekly pot will be split evenly.
  • Prizes will be awarded at the end of the season. The payout breakdown is as follows: 1st place (50%), 2nd place (30%), and 3rd place (20%)
  • In the event of a tie at the end of the season, the placements pot(s) will be split by the tied winners.

Rules

  • Picks should be submitted by kickoff of Thursday nights game.
  • If you miss the submission deadline, you can still submit picks but you will not be able to pick games that have started and you will take a loss for that game.
  • Questions? Ask The Commissioner
