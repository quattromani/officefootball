How to Play

  1. On the Home page enter your contact information.
  2. Select a winner for each game by clicking that teams logo.
  3. For the Monday night game(s); select a winner and enter the total points that you think will be scored in that game.
  4. The clear link at the bottom of the form will reset the entire form.
  5. Prizes will be awarded for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.
  6. The total points from the Monday night game(s) will be used as a tiebreaker.
  7. In the event of a tie, the first and second place pot will be split by the two winners.

Rules

  • Picks must be submitted no later than kickoff of Thursday nights game.
  • If you miss the submission deadline, you may still submit picks but you will take a loss for any game that has been started prior to submission.
