How to Play

  1. On the Picks page: select a winner for each game by clicking that teams logo.
  2. For the Monday night game(s); select a winner and enter the total points that you think will be scored in that game. (this is the tie-breaker)
  3. Enter your contact information.
  4. The RESET link at the bottom of the form will reset the entire form.

Prizes

  • 90% of the pot will be paid out weekly. 10% will go into the end-of-season pot.
  • Prize will be awarded for 1st place each week.
  • In the event of a picks tie during the regular season, the total points from the Monday night game(s) will be used to determine the winner. If a tie still exists after total points, that weekly pot will be split evenly.
  • Prizes will be awarded at the end of the season. The payout breakdown as follows: 1st place (50%), 2nd place (30%), and 3rd place (20%)
  • In the event of a tie at the end of the season, the placements pot(s) will be split by the tied winners.

Rules

  • Picks must be submitted no later than kickoff of Thursday nights game.
  • If you miss the submission deadline, you may still submit picks but you will take a loss for any game that has been started prior to submission.
